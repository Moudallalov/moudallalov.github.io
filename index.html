Let me introduce my self.

Hello, my name is Mohamad Moudallal. I'm a 15 years old teen ager born in the 00's in Beirut, Lebanon. I'm just like any other person in my age: I'm a daredevil, I like adventures, music and hate school. But, there is something I have in my life that makes me unique and different from the others.


It all started at the 7th grade.

Although I loved computers since I was 3 years old, at 7th grade it became serious. Back then, we had activities in school every Monday and Friday, I wanted to join the table tennis club, but, a new club appeared called TLE: The Little Engineer. It's a group founded in Lebanon, that teaches youth robots: building and programming them. So, I joined the group for two years and mastered Lego's NXT robots with the help of the friendly instructors. Throughout the years, I became more interested in programming, and I started learning one of the most fun and easy to understand languages: Python. I started to get to know the basics of every programming language and soon, built many Python Programs to help me with school assignments.


8th grade

After I finished the book that teaches Python, I became interested in websites, their structures and their design. With the help of Codecademy.com I started learning HTML & CSS, then I learned Javascript and jQuery, of course with the help of more books and an amazing online course on Udemy by Rob Percival.


9th grade(these days)

In the end of the summer vacation, because of my high grades that I got on the finals (8th grade), my father bought me an Apple MacBook Pro, and I decided to take advantage of it and learn iOS Development. Before the start of school, I bought an online course by my same British instructor Rob to teach me iOS Development with Apple's newest language, Swift. As many other languages I know, I started with the basics and now I think I'm obsessed with Swift. I also took some iOS design lessons by a talented instructor and designer, Meng To. With the help of these two instructors, AppCoda for their very useful tutorials and their amazing book, TeamTreeHouse for their courses about Swift, UI 8 for their UI kits and courses, the amazing projects on GitHub and the people on StackOverflow, I became one of the most talented iOS Developers at a very young age: at 14 years old, I'm a Computer Programmer, a NXT Robotic Expert, a Web Developer and an iOS Developer. Doesn't that sound great?


Any Other Interests?

Of course I'm not only addicted to Code, I have other passions. Since grade 6, I started learning graphic designing wether it's editing photos using Photoshop or creating great logos and wallpapers using Illustrator, I became a graphic designer. Since I was a child, I felled in love in Guitar. I loved the sounds and the music played using the guitar. That's why I started taking guitar lessons for a couple of years before I was getting into Graphics and Computer Science when I had to leave my guitar lessons sadly... I also love typography art and calligraphy and everything related to writing a quote with different styles using a breathtaking background. Isn't that what typography art is about? Or what I think it is?


What am I doing these days?

Learning doesn't stop! Everyday, I discover something new, wether it's about technology or science or anything that interests me. Plus, I started teaching my friends code to share the knowledge in the society. I also have my everyday routine: get up early, school, home works, sleep early...and so on.

Home Security System

The Raspberry Pi Home Security System was project done for a competition between schools. I represented my school, built a wooden house and then automated its security with the Raspberry Pi 2. I installed an LCD screen which I programmed without a module, connected to a number keypad set to enter the code to unlock the house. Inside was infrared sensors at every room entrance and a camera beside the main door, plus a buzzer which would make a loud sound in case the security system was breached.

  • Client: IEA Raspberry Pi Competition
  • Date: March 2016
  • Service: Raspberry Pi System, Hardware and Software.
